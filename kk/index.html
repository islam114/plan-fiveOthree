Uranus Overview

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and has a unique blue-green color due to methane in its atmosphere. It has 13 rings and 27 known moons.

Key Moons of Uranus:

  • Miranda: Closest large moon, 471 km diameter.
  • Ariel: Rich in water ice and silicate.
  • Umbriel: Heavily cratered, dark surface.
  • Titania: Largest moon, over 1,577 km diameter.
  • Oberon: Most distant large moon.

Rings of Uranus:

Uranus has 13 known rings, composed mainly of dark, small particles. The epsilon ring is the brightest and densest.
